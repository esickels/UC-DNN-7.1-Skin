Why Explore?

Exploring majors is an important part of attending college. Whether you have chosen a specific program or are undecided (exploratory), this module will help you review degree requirements for programs that interest you. You will also learn more about careers in different fields and hear from real-world professionals.

After completing the module, you can update your major selection when reserving your orientation. If you are still undecided, select exploratory—your advisor will ensure that you register for classes that won’t keep you from graduating on time.
